About

Career Profile

  • Associate Professor in Hindi at Ram Lal Anand College, University of Delhi, with over three decades of distinguished teaching and research experience.
  • Completed Ph.D. from the University of Delhi (2002), specializing in Drama, Theatre, and Dramatic Song-lyrics (Natak, Rangmanch, Natya Geet).
  • An accomplished scholar in Hindi literature with a focus on the intersection of theatrical performance, dramatic structure, and the lyrical traditions embedded in Hindi plays.
  • All academic degrees from BA through PhD earned from the prestigious University of Delhi, reflecting a continuous and distinguished scholarly trajectory.
