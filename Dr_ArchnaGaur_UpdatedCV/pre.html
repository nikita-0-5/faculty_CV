Presentations

Conference Presentations (Recent)

  • Kaal Kapaal Mahakaal banam Darshak aur Bazaar — A critical examination of contemporary Hindi theatre's relationship with commercial forces and the viewing public.
  • Uttar Sanrachna Vaad ki Drishti se Madhushaala — A post-structuralist reading of Harivansh Rai Bachchan's celebrated work Madhushala, exploring its layers of meaning and cultural significance.
  • Kinnar — A paper on the representation and identity of the Kinnar (transgender/third gender) community in Hindi literature and theatre.
  • Stri-Purush Sambandh: Manas aur Sambhavnaayen — An exploration of gender relations in classical Hindi texts, tracing possibilities for re-reading within a contemporary feminist framework.
  • Strivaad (Feminism in Hindi Literature) — A presentation examining the evolution of feminist thought in Hindi literary tradition.
  • Sant Kaavya Parampara — A scholarly presentation on the tradition of Saint poetry (Bhakti movement) in Hindi literary history.
  • Tapori Talk — A sociolinguistic exploration of colloquial urban Hindi idiom in contemporary dramatic and cinematic contexts.
