Teaching

Subjects Taught

  • Hindi Kahani (Hindi Short Story)
  • Hindi Nibandh evam Anya Gadya Vidhayen (Hindi Essay and Other Prose Forms)
  • Kavya Shashtra (Theory of Hindi Poetics)
  • Natak evam Rangmanch (Drama and Theatre)
  • Natya Geet (Dramatic Song-lyrics)
  • Hindi Gadya Sahitya (Hindi Prose Literature)

Teaching Philosophy

  • Committed to fostering a deep appreciation of Hindi literary traditions by connecting classical theory with contemporary performance and textual analysis.
  • Encourages students to engage with the performative dimensions of literature — understanding how written texts come alive on stage through drama and song.
  • Integrates close reading of primary texts with broader contextual frameworks drawn from Kavya Shashtra and Sanskrit poetic tradition.
